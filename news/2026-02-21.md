## Creative Platform Integration Trends

**Multi-model access** is becoming table stakes for creative platforms. The alternative to unified platforms is "tool stacking" - maintaining separate subscriptions to each specialist model.

**AI creative suites** like Raelume benefit from this fragmentation by providing unified access to the expanding model ecosystem.

**Node-based workflows** are increasingly seen as the solution for managing complex multi-model creative pipelines.
