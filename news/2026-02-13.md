# AI Model News - February 13, 2026

## Headlines

### Seedance 2.0 Goes Viral

ByteDance's new AI video model Seedance 2.0 is dominating headlines after launching February 10, 2026. The model features a 12-file multimodal input system that allows combining text prompts, reference images, and video inputs for director-level control.

Key details:
- **Duration:** 4-15 seconds per clip
- **Platform:** Limited beta on Jimeng AI
- **Global Release:** Expected February 24, 2026
- **Reception:** Comparisons to Hollywood production quality

Sources: Reuters, CNET, India Today

### Runway Raises $315M at $5.3B Valuation

Runway, maker of Gen-4.5, announced a $315M funding round, valuing the company at $5.3 billion. The raise comes after the release of Gen-4.5, their latest video generation model.

### Waymo Using Genie 3 for Self-Driving Simulation

Waymo announced they're leveraging Google DeepMind's Genie 3 to create "hyper-realistic" simulated environments for training self-driving AI. The system can simulate rare scenarios like snow on the Golden Gate Bridge.
