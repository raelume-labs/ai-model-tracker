# AI Model News - March 27, 2026

## Video Generation

### OpenAI Officially Discontinues Sora 2 Video Platform
OpenAI announced the shutdown of Sora 2 on March 24, 2026, just six months after its launch in December 2025. The standalone video generation app will cease operations effective immediately.

**Key Facts:**
- Total revenue: ~$2.1 million over 6 months
- Download trend: Declined from 3.33M peak (November) to 1.13M (February)
- Strategic pivot: Compute resources reallocated to text/reasoning models
- Disney withdrawal: $1 billion investment plan canceled

**Official Statement:** OpenAI cited "rising compute demand" and focus on "world simulation to advance robotics" as primary reasons for the discontinuation.

**Market Impact:** The shutdown leaves Seedance 2.0, Grok Imagine, and Kling 3.0 as the dominant video generation platforms, with Seedance 2.0 currently leading leaderboards.

**API Status:** The Video API launched March 13, 2026 for developers remains active, though future support is unclear.

**Competitive Context:** ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 outperformed Sora in native 2K resolution, generation speed (~30s vs 60-120s), and multimodal capabilities with 4-input-type support.
