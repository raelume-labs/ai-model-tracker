# AI Model News - March 26, 2026

## Video Generation

### Seedance 2.0 Global Launch Status Update
ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 has officially completed its global launch rollout as of March 2026, moving from limited beta to full availability. The model now leads both text-to-video (Elo: 1269) and image-to-video (Elo: 1351) categories on Artificial Analysis blind-vote leaderboards.

**Key Features Confirmed:**
- Unified multimodal architecture supporting up to 9 images, 3 video clips, and 3 audio clips as references
- Audio-video joint generation with synchronized output
- Director-level control interface designed for professional workflows
- Industrial-grade content creation capabilities with improved physical accuracy and motion stability

**Market Position:** Seedance 2.0's leaderboard dominance represents a significant shift in the video generation landscape, challenging previous leaders like Grok Imagine and Runway Gen-4.5 in multimodal input capabilities.
