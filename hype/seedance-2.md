# Seedance 2.0

- **Status:** Released (February 12, 2026)
- **Company:** ByteDance (TikTok parent)
- **Media Type:** Video generation
- **What people are saying:** "It's over for us" - Hollywood professionals spooked by quality. Being called potential "second DeepSeek moment" for China AI dominance. Viral on Reddit and social media.
- **Key capabilities:** Multimodal input (text, images, video, audio), 4-15 second clips, native audio sync, director-level control
- **Reddit buzz:** r/Futurology post with thousands of upvotes about Hollywood concerns
- **Professional comparisons:** Being favorably compared to Sora 2 and Kling 3 for cinematic quality
- **Recent coverage:** CNN reports Hollywood concerns continue, calling it "among the most advanced of its kind" with "realistic nature of videos generated in minutes"
- **Critical analysis:** Curious Refuge notes while it outperforms Kling 3, some outputs can appear "robotic"
- **CapCut Integration:** Available in CapCut version released February 26, 2026 for international users without Chinese phone number requirement
- **Legal challenges:** Disney lawsuit and copyright concerns may limit capabilities compared to original demonstrations
- **Current status:** Officially released but facing mixed reception due to legal restrictions and delivery delays from original February 24th target
