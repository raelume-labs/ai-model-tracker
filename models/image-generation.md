## Model Details

### Qwen Image 2.0
- **Developer:** Alibaba
- **Release:** February 10, 2026
- **Parameters:** 7B
- **Max Resolution:** 2K (native)
- **Speed:** ~15 seconds
- **Strengths:** #1 ranked on AI Arena blind evaluation, competitive quality
- **Status:** Quietly released, gaining momentum

### Flux 2 Pro Ultra
- **Developer:** Black Forest Labs
- **Max Resolution:** 2048px
- **Speed:** ~5 seconds
- **Strengths:** Style versatility, extensive community support
- **Available via:** Replicate, Fal.ai, Krea, Raelume

### Nano Banana Pro (Gemini Image)
- **Developer:** Google
- **Max Resolution:** 4K (4096px)
- **Speed:** ~45 seconds
- **Strengths:** Reasoning model, excellent prompt adherence
- **Available via:** Google AI Studio, Krea, Raelume, Freepik

### Imagen 4
- **Developer:** Google
- **Speed:** ~60 seconds (Fast variant: ~20s)
- **Strengths:** Superior typography, photorealism
- **Variants:** Imagen 4, Imagen 4 Ultra, Imagen 4 Fast

### Ideogram 3.0
- **Developer:** Ideogram
- **Speed:** ~30 seconds
- **Strengths:** Typography handling, graphic design

### DALL-E 3
- **Developer:** OpenAI
- **Integration:** Native in ChatGPT, API available
- **Strengths:** Natural language understanding, safety guardrails

### Midjourney v6.1
- **Developer:** Midjourney
- **Platform:** Discord-based, web app in beta
- **Strengths:** Artistic quality, coherent character generation
