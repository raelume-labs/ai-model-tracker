## Voice Generation

### ElevenLabs V3
- **Developer:** ElevenLabs
- **Features:**
  - Voice cloning from short samples
  - 29+ languages supported
  - Real-time voice synthesis
  - Professional dubbing quality
- **Available via:** API, Raelume, Freepik Spaces

### Voice Cloning Comparison

| Feature | ElevenLabs | Play.ht | Resemble AI |
|---------|------------|---------|-------------|
| Clone Quality | Excellent | Very Good | Very Good |
| Languages | 29+ | 140+ | Multiple |
| Real-time | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Free Tier | Yes | Yes | Limited |

## Music Generation

### Suno v4
- **Developer:** Suno AI
- **Features:**
  - Full song generation (vocals + instruments)
  - Genre flexibility
  - Lyrics input or generation
  - Up to 4 minutes per generation
- **Use Cases:** Background music, demo tracks, creative exploration

### Udio
- **Developer:** Udio
- **Features:**
  - Music generation with genre control
  - Stem separation
  - Extend/remix features

### Comparison Notes

- **Best for Voice:** ElevenLabs (quality and features)
- **Best for Songs:** Suno (full production quality)
- **Best for Sound Effects:** Stable Audio, ElevenLabs Sound Effects
- **Open Source Option:** MusicGen by Meta
